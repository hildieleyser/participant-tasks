Online Ball-Tossing Game

Welcome! In this study, you will play an online ball-tossing game called Cyberball with two other participants who are also doing this study right now.


The purpose of this task is to study mental visualization during a simple online game. We want you to imagine the experience as vividly as possible — picture yourself tossing and catching the ball in real time.


You will play three short games (about 1-2 minutes each). Between games, we will ask you a few brief questions about your experience.

How to Play

  • You and two other players will toss a ball back and forth.
  • When you receive the ball, click on the player you want to throw it to.
  • When another player has the ball, just wait for them to decide.
  • There are no right or wrong choices — just play naturally.

The game will begin once all players are connected. Please wait a moment after clicking "Ready".
